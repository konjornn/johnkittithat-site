Make ads people don't skip.

John is a director at Songmue Production, bringing creativity and vision to every project — making ads that customers easily remember and never forget, in his own unique way.

He likes to explore storytelling with new techniques — VFX, camera movement, visual design — so people don't skip his ads. His ideas live on upcoming trends and always reflect back to branding recognition.

Representing the key selling point clearly and sensationally, he helps brands promote sales and make ads run the world — getting to the point and going viral.

Most of all, he likes to make people happy working with him, exploring new ways of work with passion. He helps teams create something new, without limitation, always thinking of ways to push the work further than before.
